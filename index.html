FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the VS Ultimate Player.

Is VS Ultimate Player completely free?

Yes, it is 100% free and open-source. There are no advertisements, subscription fees, or hidden telemetry trackers. It operates completely offline and is designed by Viraj Sanjeewa for high-performance offline media playback.

What file formats does it support?

VS Ultimate Player supports virtually all popular audio and video formats, including MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, WEBM, MP3, FLAC, AAC, WAV, and high-fidelity 4K UHD videos with H.265/HEVC hardware decoding support.

Does it require installation?

No, it is a fully portable executable player. You do not need to run an installer or grant administrative access. Simply download the `.exe` file, double-click to launch, and start playing your media instantly.

How do I load and style subtitles?

You can drag and drop any subtitle file (.srt, .vtt, .ass, etc.) directly into the player window. By right-clicking during video playback, you can customize the font style, color, size, positioning, and subtitle synchronization delay on the fly.
