# VS Ultimate Media Player v2.0
A premium, feature-rich web-based media player designed for ultimate control over your audio and video experience.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎬 Advanced Video Playback
- Universal Support: Play video and audio files with a sleek, glassmorphic interface.
- Screen Transformations: Zoom in/out, Mirror (Horizontal/Vertical), and change Aspect Ratio (Fit, Stretch, Original).
- Playback Speed: Control speed from 0.25x to 4x without losing pitch.
- A-B Repeat: Loop specific sections of any video or audio track.
- Auto-Resume: Remembers exactly where you left off for every file.

### 💬 Premium Subtitle System
- Extreme Customization: Change font size, color, family, and styles (Bold/Italic) in real-time.
- Interactive Dragging: Right-click the handle to toggle, then move subtitles anywhere on the screen (X & Y axis).
- Auto-Lift: In fullscreen, subtitles automatically lift up when player controls appear so they never get covered.
- Sync Adjustment: Fine-tune subtitle timing with dedicated delay/advance controls.
- Wide Format Support: Supports .vtt, .srt, .ass, .ssa, .sub, .lrc, and many more.

### 🎵 Audio & Visuals
- Dynamic Visualizer: Beautiful circular audio visualizer that reacts to the music.
- 10-Band Equalizer: Built-in EQ with presets (Bass Boost, Pop, etc.) to customize your sound.
- OSD Indicators: Modern on-screen displays for Volume, Brightness, and Seeking.

### 📁 Playlist & Library
- Persistent Playlists: Create, save, and manage custom playlists that stay even after refreshing the page.
- Quick Add: Right-click any playing file and add it to your preferred playlist instantly.
- Smart Drag & Drop: Drop entire folders or multiple files to load them into the library.

### 🖱️ User Experience (UX)
- KMPlayer Style Menu: Pro-level right-click context menu for quick access to all features.
- Advanced Hotkeys:
    - Space: Play/Pause
    - Arrow Up/Down: Volume
    - Arrow Left/Right: Seek 10s
    - B / V: Increase/Decrease Brightness
    - Enter: Toggle Fullscreen
    - S: Stop
- Settings Panel: Customize the player's primary theme color, background blur, and default subtitle behavior.

## 🚀 How to Use
1. Open the index.html in any modern browser.
2. Drag & Drop your media files into the player area.
3. Right-click on the player to explore advanced controls.
4. Enjoy a premium cinematic experience!
